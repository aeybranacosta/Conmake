This is a certified conlang classic.
Welcome to ConMake! You’ve just stumbled upon the ultimate workshop for linguistic architects. Whether you’re a veteran world-builder or just someone who wants to doodle a secret alphabet to hide notes from your siblings, you’re in the right place.
In a world full of boring fonts and standard keyboards, ConMake is here to bring back the art of the hand-drawn script. We don't believe in "typing" a language; we believe in crafting it. From the messy scrawl of a swamp-dwelling tribe to the geometric precision of a space-faring empire, ConMake is where your symbols become legendary.
🖋️ What is ConMake?
ConMake is a streamlined, browser-based workspace that turns your mouse or stylus into a linguistic tool. By using a strict (Word)=(Drawing) logic, you can systematically build a dictionary of 123 unique characters.
It's not about being "professional"—it's about the vibe. ConMake gives you exactly 123 slots to prove your language has what it takes to be a certified classic. What are the 123 most important concepts in your world? It’s time to draw them.
🚀 Core Features
1. The ConMake Protocol
Everything starts with a name. Hit that + Make Language button to kick off a new project. Give your language a name that sounds like it was pulled from a dusty history book (or a futuristic data-pad).
2. The 123-Slot Lexicon Grid
The heart of every classic is its structure. We give you 123 slots to map out your lexicon:
The Definition: Type your word in the box (e.g., "Fire," "Friend," or "Taxation").
The Inscription: Draw the character that represents that concept.
Non-Linear Creativity: You don't have to start at Slot 1. If you have a great idea for Slot 69, go for it.
3. Precision Drawing Canvases
Each slot is powered by its own HTML5 Canvas, meaning your drawings stay exactly where you put them.
Smooth Input: Designed to feel responsive whether you're using a mouse or a touchscreen.
The "C" Reset: Even classics have rough drafts. If your symbol looks more like a potato than a dragon, hit the C button to wipe it and try again.
Save Logic: Lock in your glyphs as you go to ensure your language is preserved.
🛠️ Technical Breakdown
ConMake is built to run on anything with a browser. No downloads, no bloat, just code.
HTML5/CSS3: A clean, focused UI with a "Sticky Header" so you never lose your place while scrolling down to slot 123.
JavaScript (ES6): The engine under the hood that manages all 123 canvases simultaneously.
GitHub Pages Ready: Host it for free, share the link, and let the world see your certified classic.
📖 How to Get Started
Open ConMake: Fire up the index.html.
Name Your Legacy: Click + Make Language and name your project.
Map it Out: Type a word in a slot.
Draw it In: Create the symbol for that word.
Save: Click Save to commit the character.
Complete the Set: Fill all 123 slots to reach "Classic" status.
